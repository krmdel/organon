## Why presentations feel the same

Most decks rely on a template that controls **only color and font**, not layout. The result: every slide has the same shape. The audience stops seeing the *structure* of your argument — they see wallpaper.

A real template carries **layout archetypes**. Title slides breathe. Stats slides punch. Quotes center. Figures dominate.

*This deck is built on that idea.*
